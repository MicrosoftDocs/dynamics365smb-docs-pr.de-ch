Wählen Sie dann den zugehörigen Link aus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9641d-111">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Contacts**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
